# BARL: Free Overlay

Overview page for BARL, the free Rocket League broadcasting overlay. BARL adds an automated series score tracker, real-time team boost meters, and customizable team names and logos to any stream. It works with OBS Studio, Twitch, YouTube, and other major platforms.

**[View the live page](https://lndrlndr.github.io/BARL-Overlay/)** · [Full product page](https://lndrlndr.github.io/BARL/) · [Download BARL](https://lndrlndr.github.io/BARL_App/)

## Features

- Automated series score tracking
- Team boost meters pulled straight from Rocket League at low latency
- Custom team names and logos through a Control Panel
- Works while spectating, playing, or watching replays
- Hide or reposition logos for a minimal look
- Premium plan adds community-designed themes and full customization
